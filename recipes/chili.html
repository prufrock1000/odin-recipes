Vegetarian Bean Chili

Description

A classic chili from Central America served up with a vegetarian twist

Ingredients

  • Green pepper diced
  • Onion diced
  • Garlic finely chopped
  • tinned black beans
  • other beans of your choice
  • tinned tomatoes
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • salt

Method

  1. heat oil in large pan add onions and green peppers and fry until brown
  2. add garlic and cook for 1 minute
  3. add beans and spices
  4. add tinned tomatoes and cook for 2 hours on low
