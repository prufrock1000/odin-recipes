Green Lentil Dal

Description

Created by the noble people of Kerala and made using the freshest green lentils and smoothest coconut milk, this modern spin on an indian classic is sure to become a family favourite.

This Indian green lentils recipe is easy to make with a lot of ingredients that you might already have at home. Or, these ingredients are easily found at most supermarkets. No need to visit an Asian market for any of them!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, diced
  • red onion diced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk (well mixed)
  • 1 cup dry green lentils
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Dash of crushed red chili pepper
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • chopped coriander for garnish

Method

  1. Heat a small to medium-sized dutch oven or saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Once warm, heat the vegetable oil.
  3. gently fry whole spices
  4. once fragrant add onion and fry until soft
  5. add garlic and ginger and rest of spices
  6. add lentils coconut milk and stock gently simmer until lentils are cooked through
  7. garnish with chopped coriander
